About Production
The corporation "Automir" was founded in 2006, Slovakia by former employees of the largest tire enterprises in Ukraine, PA "Dneproshina" and PA "Rosava" on the territory of the former USSR. The head-office is located in the city of Trencin. The Trenčín Region is a tire region thanks to such industrial tire giants as Matador, Vipo, Konstrukta industry and others. It has been actively developing, gaining experience, creating partnerships and strengthening its position in the market. Gradually, the market situation began to change, there was a shortage of individual tire sizes and an urgent need to provide partner-enterprises with the required tire sizes. This is how the brand “UkrTechProm”, “SuperTechProm”, “RosShinProm” appeared. Tires of our own design are produced on the basis of the facilities of the enterprises "Rosava" and "Voltyer-Prom". In 2010, the first own agricultural tire, size 23.1R26, was produced and successfully supplied to the EU. And this was only the first step, as the market was already demanding further moves to expand the number of manufactured sizes. The purpose wasn't easy, but the corporation was staffed with qualified tire specialists, so it was achieved. At this time, the production of tires 21.3R24, 16 / 70-20 begins. Currently, 14 types of tires are produced, such as passenger, agricultural, truck tires (including tires with adjustable pressure) as well as industrial tires. Our tires have proven themselves in the markets of Ukraine, Russia, in Europe (Bulgaria, Germany, Hungary, France, the Netherlands) in China and Egypt. Automir s.r.o. is a European partner of the Ukrainian tire corporation “Ukrteсhprom”, founded in 1993
