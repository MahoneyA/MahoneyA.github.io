About Us
The Ukrtekhprom Corporation have been founded in 1993 in the Dnepropetrovsk city and began activities as a tire market operator, generally promoting the products of Dneproshina JSC. The company group of Ukrtechprom Corporation began to work actively and gaining experience and creating partnerships, entrench the position on market and win prestige among customers. The market situation has begun to change eventually. There was a lack of individual tire sizes. There was an urgent need to provide the fellow partner enterprises (KhTZ, KrAZ) with required tire sizes. There was a startup at the production of tyres under its own brand. As a result the UTP brand come through and tyres under this brand started to produce based on the capacities of the Rosava PJSC enterprise. In 2010 was produced the first tire size 23.1R26. But this was only the first step. The market already demanded a moving forward to expand the number of manufactured sizes. It seemed to be the difficult task. But corporation have been staffed by a qualified tyre production experts and the task was completed. Begins the production tires 21.3R24, 18.00-25.16/70-20. 14 tyre sizes currently are producing includ such as: automobile, agricultural, freight, freight with adjustable pressure, industrial tires. Tires have proved themselves in Ukraine, Russia and Europe (Bulgaria, Germany, Hungary, France, the Netherlands) markets, as well as in the Asia regions (China) and the Middle East. The new tubeless tire preparations are being made to launch for tractor trailers and trailed agricultural machines 500/50R17 tire size. There are a lot of plans for the further development of a new assortment of tires cover various industries. Taking into account the needs of the market and the capabilities of the manufacturer in 2020 it is planned to produce 4 more tire sizes. Now the tyres saleing in Ukraine is carried out through a central warehouse in the Dnipro city and branch network. For customer convenience and prompt delivery all tyre sizes can be purchased through the corporation online store - UTP.COM.UA. Besides that in 2018 basis of the Voltyr-Prom enterprise (Volzhsky city, Russia) have been started production of tyres under the brands SUPERTESHPROM and ROSHINPROM 23.1R26 and 6L-12. Also in prosess of preparing to the production the tyres sizes like 30.5LR-32, 21.3R24 and 28.1-25. In Belgorod work a branch of "Rosshinprom" LLC .
About us
The corporation "Automir" was founded in 2006, Slovakia by former employees of the largest tire enterprises in Ukraine, PA "Dneproshina" and PA "Rosava" on the territory of the former USSR. The head-office is located in the city of Trencin. The Trenčín Region is a tire region thanks to such industrial tire giants as Matador, Vipo, Konstrukta industry and others. It has been actively developing, gaining experience, creating partnerships and strengthening its position in the market.
