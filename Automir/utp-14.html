About us
The corporation "Automir" was founded in 2006, Slovakia by former employees of the largest tire enterprises in Ukraine, PA "Dneproshina" and PA "Rosava" on the territory of the former USSR. The head-office is located in the city of Trencin. The Trenčín Region is a tire region thanks to such industrial tire giants as Matador, Vipo, Konstrukta industry and others. It has been actively developing, gaining experience, creating partnerships and strengthening its position in the market.
